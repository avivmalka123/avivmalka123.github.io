Your Lock Won't Save You

Disc locks, chain locks, handlebar locks — they all do one thing: they make honest people feel better. They do not recover stolen bikes.

Professional thieves don't pick locks. They pull up in a van, lift your bike off the ground, and drive away. Your chain gets cut in under 10 seconds with a battery-powered angle grinder. Your disc lock? It goes in the van with the bike.

Locks are a deterrent for opportunists. Against a professional crew, they are theater. The only thing that works after the theft begins is knowing exactly where your bike is, in real time.
